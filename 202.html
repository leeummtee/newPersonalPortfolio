Video Effects & Editing

creating both a short visual efffects edit & a found-footage Toronto Raptors trailer video.

01. summary

objective

These videos were made for a New Media Images class. For the special effects assignment, students were given a one-week timeframe to implement at least two kinds of visual effects in a short video. For the final project, students were given different options for the video and had three weeks to complete it. I chose to do a found-footage trailer.

programs used

Adobe After Effects and Premier Pro

02. the details

a. effects video

I wanted to make the theme about basketball since I have always been into the sport, so the base footage is a clip I filmed of a friend dribbling a ball and taking a shot. In terms of the effects, I wanted to make it as engaging as possible so I decided to make one using glowing-line effects like that ones that have become popular on social media. The other two effects that I incorporated are a screen replacement and an explosion effect. These were all done in After Effects and Premier Pro.

b. found-footage trailer

I decided to make my trailer about the Toronto Raptors since they are my favourite basketball team. I wanted the video to showcase their run during the 2018-2019 season, from the regular season to the NBA finals. To do so, I found several clips on YouTube and edited them all together in Premier Pro.

03. reflection

The effects project was a fun opportunity for me to learn more about After Effects and how to bring videos to life through creativity. I especially enjoyed the trailer project, as I really loved being able to make a video about a topic and team that I am passionate about. My efforts for both projects were rewarded as I received full marks on the effects video, and nominations for best sound design and best found-footage trailer for my Toronto Raptors video.
